Code of Conduct

Welcome to the RustTechX Summit 2024! To ensure a safe, inclusive, and productive environment for all participants, we've established this Code of Conduct. It applies to all attendees, speakers, sponsors, and organizers, both in-person and online. Our goal is to foster collaboration and respect within our community.

Our Rules

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment. All participants must adhere to the following:

  • We encourage digital and hybrid networking to connect our global community.
  • We foster a nondiscriminatory and nonjudgmental space for all.
  • We do not tolerate any form of sexism or racism.
  • We do not tolerate spamming or promotional content in live feeds or chats.

Our Community Values

At RustTechX, we champion:

  • Inclusivity: Welcoming diverse perspectives and backgrounds.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative and technical excellence in Rust.
  • Collaboration: Building a supportive community for learning and growth.

Consequences of Inappropriate Behavior

Participants asked to stop any inappropriate behavior are expected to comply immediately. If a participant engages in behavior that violates this code of conduct, the conference organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the conference with no refund.

Need Help?

If you experience or notice any inappropriate behavior (as described above), please reach out immediately:

  • Email: conduct@rusttechx.org
  • On-site: Approach any staff member wearing a "RustTechX Organizer" badge.
  • Reports can be made anonymously, and we aim to respond within 24 hours.
