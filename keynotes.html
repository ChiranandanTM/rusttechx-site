Keynote Sessions

All keynotes take place in the Auditorium K. Each keynote will last between 10 and 15 minutes. These sessions are delivered by industry leaders, community experts, and innovators across domains, offering insights into the future of open-source technologies and practical applications of Rust in real-world environments.
