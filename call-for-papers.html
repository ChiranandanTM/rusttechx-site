Call for Papers - RustTechX Summit 2024

Join us at RustTechX Summit 2024 to share your expertise in Rust programming and its applications! Submit your presentation proposal and be part of a vibrant community driving innovation.

Why Present at RustTechX Summit?

The RustTechX Summit is a premier event for Rust enthusiasts, developers, and industry leaders to explore cutting-edge applications of the Rust programming language. By presenting, you will:

  • Showcase your work to a global audience of professionals and enthusiasts
  • Contribute to the Rust community by sharing insights and innovations
  • Network with experts from Bosch and other leading organizations
  • Inspire the next generation of Rust developers

Submission Guidelines

We invite authors to submit a presentation abstract to the Team Mailbox. Your proposal should include:

  • Presentation Title: A concise and descriptive title for your talk
  • Speaker Details: Name(s) of speaker(s) and co-speaker(s), including phone number and email address. The main speaker should be listed first. Each speaker should provide a bio (up to 500 characters)
  • Keywords: 3-5 relevant keywords that align with your presentation topic
  • Abstract: A brief summary of your presentation, covering methods, results, and motivation (up to 1300 characters, including spaces)

Note: The conference language is English. Presentations are allocated 20 minutes, followed by a 5-minute Q&A session.

Topic Suggestions

We welcome presentations on a wide range of Rust-related topics, including but not limited to:

Rust in automotive and embedded systems
High-performance computing with Rust
Safety-critical software development
Rust for web and backend development
Tooling and ecosystem advancements
Case studies and real-world applications

Submission Process

Prepare your proposal as a PDF or text document and send it to the Team Mailbox. Ensure all required details are included to facilitate the review process. Accepted speakers will be notified via email and provided with further instructions.

Questions? If you have any questions about the submission process or the RustTechX Summit, please reach out to us via the Contact Page.
