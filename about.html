RustTechX Summit by BGSW: Pioneering Rust from Chip to Cloud

The RustTechX Summit, hosted by Rust team at Bosch Global Software Technologies (BGSW), is a premier event for Rust enthusiasts, developers, and industry leaders. It's the place to explore cutting-edge innovations and best practices in Rust, with a focus on embedded systems, cloud computing, and beyond.
